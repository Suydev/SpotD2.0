Built for music hoarders.

Every detail tuned for speed, polish, and total ownership of your collection.

🔎

Catalog-wide search

Type any artist, song, album, or playlist. Cover art, year, and track counts up front — no copy-pasting Spotify links.

⚡

4× parallel downloads

Configurable workers grab tracks in parallel. A 25-song album finishes in under a minute on a decent connection.

🎨

Tagged & gorgeous

Each MP3 ships with embedded cover art, year, album, artist, and synced lyrics where available. Drop into any player.

📱

QR for mobile

Every finished ZIP gets a QR code. Scan it from your phone, file lands instantly. Perfect for AirDrop replacements.

🗄️

3-day retention

Files stick around for 72 hours so you can re-download from a new device. After that, they auto-clean to keep things tidy.

🛡️

Restart-safe sessions

Your download history persists across restarts. Crashes, deploys, reboots — your library stays put.
